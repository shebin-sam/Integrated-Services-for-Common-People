Indian Railways

IRCTC, or the Indian Railway Catering and Tourism Corporation, is a subsidiary of the Indian Railways that handles online ticketing, catering, and tourism operations. In addition to ticket booking, IRCTC offers a variety of services such as e-catering, where passengers can order meals to be delivered to their seats, and tourism packages that include rail tours, hotels, and travel insurance.
